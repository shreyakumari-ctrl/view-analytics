About MyTube Studio

Learn more about our mission and vision

MyTube Studio

A creator-centric analytics dashboard designed to empower content creators with actionable insights into their channel performance.

Built as a modern web application, MyTube Studio provides an intuitive interface for monitoring views, engagement, revenue, and audience growth. We believe every creator deserves access to powerful analytics tools that help them understand their audience and grow their channels.

Our Mission

To democratize analytics for creators by providing an easily accessible, feature-rich platform that helps content creators make data-driven decisions and grow their channels.

Why MyTube Studio?

Real-time Insights

Track your performance metrics as they happen with live data updates and instant notifications.

User-Friendly Interface

Intuitive design inspired by industry-leading analytics platforms for seamless navigation.

Comprehensive Analytics

From basic metrics to advanced audience insights, get everything you need in one place.

Privacy-Focused

Your data stays with you. We prioritize your privacy and data security.

Always Improving

Actively developed with creator feedback to continuously enhance the experience.

Open Source

Built on open-source principles, customizable and transparent for community contributions.
